Import an Archidekt collection

Archidekt's collection export is a straightforward CSV. SpellControl reads it directly — the more columns you include in the export, the more per-copy detail survives the trip.

Export from Archidekt

  1. On archidekt.com, open your Collection.
  2. Choose the collection's Export option (CSV).
  3. If the exporter lets you pick columns, include at least Name, Edition, and Quantity — add Collector Number, Condition, Language, Foil, and Price for full fidelity.

Import into SpellControl

  1. Open SpellControl — the add-cards panel opens.
  2. Drop the CSV in, or paste its contents.
  3. Review the resolved cards and confirm the import.

The format is auto-detected from the file's headers.

What SpellControl reads from an Archidekt export

  • Printing — name + edition, plus collector number when the column is present.
  • Quantity per card.
  • Finish — foil, etched, or nonfoil.
  • Condition — NM / LP / MP / HP / DMG codes normalize to SpellControl's condition scale.
  • Language.
  • Price, kept on the card as its purchase price.
  • Category or tags — kept as each card's source category.

Notes

  • Archidekt deck exports in Arena text format also import — see the Arena & text lists guide.
  • Rows without an edition still import; SpellControl picks a printing, and you can fix any copy's printing afterwards.
